LATEST ANNOUNCEMENTS

Library Extended Hours During Midterms

The university library will extend its operating hours from 7:00 AM to 2:00 AM during the midterm examination period (May 1-12, 2025). Additional study rooms have been made available for group study sessions.

Posted by: Academic Affairs Office • April 15, 2025

Research Symposium Registration Now Open

Registration for the Annual Undergraduate Research Symposium is now open. Students interested in presenting their research projects should submit their abstracts by April 30, 2025. This year's symposium will feature keynote speaker Dr. Eleanor Richards from Cambridge University.

Posted by: Research Department • April 12, 2025

Campus Sustainability Initiative Launch

The University is proud to announce the launch of our new Campus Sustainability Initiative. Starting next month, we will implement comprehensive recycling programs, energy-efficient lighting across campus, and introduce electric vehicle charging stations in all parking areas.

Posted by: Facilities Management • April 10, 2025
